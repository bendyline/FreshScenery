fly the beautiful world

Fresh Scenery Autogen adds fresh, accurate scenery and structures to your photo-real satellite/aerial imagery for Flight Simulator X, Flight Simulator X Steam Edition, and Prepar3D™.

Sourced from multiple high-resolution data sources and always-up-to-date OpenStreetMaps data, you'll get the most realistic large-scale simulator experience possible. Fresh Scenery products are also completely free. Enjoy!
